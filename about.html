Our Story

Neighborhood Detailing was born from a simple passion: making every car look and feel like it just rolled off the showroom floor. What started as a weekend hobby has grown into Newark's most trusted mobile detailing service.

Founded in 2020 by auto enthusiast Rakib, our company began with a single goal - to provide professional-grade detailing services that rival the best shops, but with the convenience of coming directly to our customers. We believe that everyone deserves to drive a clean, beautiful vehicle, and we're here to make that happen.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers throughout Newark and the surrounding areas, building lasting relationships one detail at a time. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us the neighborhood's go-to choice for premium auto detailing services.

Certified Detailer

Meet Rakib

Owner & Master Detailer

Rakib's journey into the world of auto detailing began over a decade ago when he restored his first classic car. What started as a personal project quickly became an obsession with achieving the perfect finish. After years of honing his craft and learning from industry professionals, Rakib decided to share his expertise with his community.

"I believe that every car tells a story, and my job is to help that story shine," says Rakib. "Whether it's a daily driver that needs some TLC or a luxury vehicle that demands perfection, I approach every project with the same level of dedication and attention to detail."
